Strategic Brief

Quest Body

Purpose

Move workflow assignment from the cartographer to the downstream skills that actually execute the work, ensuring each tactician and quick stamps its workflow identity in the campaign manifest on first contact.

Desired Outcome

liang-quest-general-tactician, liang-quest-tdd-tactician, and liang-quest-quick all stamp workflow in the campaign manifest on contact. Tacticians validate their own stamp post-write as defense-in-depth layer 1.

Scope Boundary

Changes to three SKILL.md files (general-tactician, tdd-tactician, quick). Manifest-level stamping only. No changes to how tacticians plan or how quick scouts and executes.

Victory Conditions

  • liang-quest-general-tactician/SKILL.md includes instructions to stamp workflow: general in the campaign manifest
  • liang-quest-tdd-tactician/SKILL.md includes instructions to stamp workflow: tdd in the campaign manifest
  • liang-quest-quick/SKILL.md includes instructions to stamp workflow: quick in the campaign manifest
  • All three skills write workflow to the manifest only, never to quest contract files
  • Tacticians include a post-stamp validation step that re-reads the manifest to confirm the stamp landed

Depends On

  • q001 — Establish Schema v2 in Quest Core

Boss Board / Risks

  • Tactician stamping bug (High) — Quest gets planned but workflow not stamped in manifest. Executor silently skips it. Mitigated by post-stamp validation.
  • Coordinated update (Medium) — Three files need coordinated updates; partial update leaves inconsistent state.

Fog of War / Open Questions

  • How the tactician's post-stamp validation should fail (error message format, whether it blocks the next quest or aborts the chain)
